Startup News: xAI in Talks to Raise $10 Billion, Dell Nears $5 Billion AI Server Deal

The startup world is being shaken by recent news, and **xAI**, Elon Musk's artificial intelligence (AI) company, is at the center of attention. Here’s a summary of the latest developments:

## xAI Potentially Valued at $75 Billion

Reports indicate that **xAI**, the AI startup led by Elon Musk, is in talks to raise **$10 billion** in a new funding round. According to Bloomberg News, this deal could value the company at an astonishing **$75 billion** [^1]. This announcement, highlighted by StartupNews.fyi, reflects the massive investor interest and capital flow into the AI sector.

This figure not only demonstrates investors' confidence in xAI’s future but also underscores the growing impact of artificial intelligence across various industries.

## Dell Nearing Deal to Sell AI-Optimized Servers to xAI

In another development related to xAI, **Dell Technologies** is close to finalizing a deal worth over **$5 billion** to supply AI-optimized servers to the startup. This move, also reported by Bloomberg News and highlighted by StartupNews.fyi, represents a significant investment in infrastructure to support xAI’s AI initiatives [^2].

Acquiring these servers will enable xAI to strengthen its computational capabilities and accelerate the development of advanced AI solutions, further establishing itself as a key player in this rapidly growing field.

## Conclusion

These developments indicate a significant effort by xAI to expand its technical capabilities and compete in the dynamic AI landscape. Stay tuned for more updates on how this startup continues to shape the future of technology.
